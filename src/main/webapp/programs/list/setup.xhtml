Here is what you can do with your lists:

Create custom fields

Collect customized information from your subscribers like multiple names and email addresses, referrals, groupings, tags, etc. These information can be used to select campaign targets later.

Lead generation channel

Think of your lists as different sources of leads coming from different channels. If you have a CRM, blog or e-commerce site, you could sync them into Segmail and blast campaigns at a cheaper rate.

Minimal coding

Set redirects upon certain events like signup, confirmation and unsubscribe, without the use of API programming. Define specific field values like email, names, addresses, preferences, etc, which you can pass it back to your CRM, blogs or e-commerce store.

Define external datasource

Using just your database login credentials, you can rely on our synchronization engine to keep your subscriber's info up-to-date with your main apps.
